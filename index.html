Features

Built for people who stay in deep focus

Calm but reliable — alerts that find you without breaking your flow.

Visual alert before meetings

A polished overlay appears in your open tabs minutes before the meeting starts — clear, calm, impossible to miss.

Stronger highlight when it's time

The alert intensifies when a meeting has already started, so you always know the difference between "coming up" and "now".

One-click join

A quick join button in the alert takes you straight to the meeting link — no hunting through calendar tabs.

Snooze, dismiss, or minimize

Full control over each alert. Snooze for a few minutes, dismiss it, or minimize to a small corner indicator.

Optional sound alert

Enable a subtle audio cue and test it right from the popup — so you know exactly what to expect before a meeting.

Fully configurable

Adjust the overlay style, visual effects, and alert timing. Available in Portuguese, English, and Spanish.
